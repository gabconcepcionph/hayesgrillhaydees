About Hay-Es Grill (Haydee's)

Hay-Es Grill, also known as Haydee's Kitchenette, is a beloved Filipino restaurant serving the San Jose del Monte community. Located along National Road in Gaya-Gaya, we offer a warm and welcoming atmosphere where families and friends can enjoy authentic Filipino cuisine.

Our kitchen specializes in traditional Filipino dishes, with our famous kare-kare, succulent steaks, and fresh grilled fish being customer favorites. The friendly staff works hard to ensure every guest feels at home, while our cool atmosphere makes every dining experience memorable.
